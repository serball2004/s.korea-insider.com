Here are some cool things you can try in korea!

1.Enjoy Delicous korean Food. In South korea there is a lot of street foods, food stalls, restaurants that you should try. Don't miss out their best foods when you travel here in korea! check this three famous korean food.

Samgyupsal

Samgyupsal (also spelled "samgyeopsal") is a popular Korean dish made with grilled pork belly. The word "samgyupsal" is derived from two Korean words: "samgyeop" which means "pork belly" and "sal" which means "meat."
In Korea, samgyupsal is typically served with various side dishes such as lettuce, kimchi, and other pickled vegetables. The meat is often cut into bite-sized pieces and wrapped in lettuce leaves, along with other ingredients such as garlic, green onions, and chili paste. It is a popular dish to enjoy with friends and family, often accompanied by beer or soju (a Korean alcoholic beverage).

Street Food

Tteokbokki, Odeng, Hotteok, Japchae, Sundae, Mandu, Twigim, Gyeran-ppang These are just a few examples of the many delicious street foods you can find in South Korea. Street food is an integral part of the culture and cuisine in South Korea and is a must-try for anyone visiting the country.

Ramen

In South Korea, ramen is often called "ramyeon" (라면) and is a popular convenience food that can be found in most grocery stores, convenience stores, and restaurants. Some popular ramyeon brands in South Korea include Shin Ramyun, Neoguri, and Jin Ramen. These instant ramyeon packages come with pre-packaged noodles and a flavor packet that can be mixed with boiling water to make a quick and easy meal. Jjambbong (짬뽕), Budae jjigae (부대찌개), Ramyeon carbonara, Cheese ramyeon.Ramen has become a beloved comfort food in South Korea and is enjoyed by people of all ages.

2. You can visit the various temples in Korea, they showcase their histories, even the old towns in Korea.

Temples

South Korea has a rich history of Buddhism, and as a result, it has many beautiful temples that showcase the country's unique cultural heritage. Here are some notable temples in South Korea: Bulguksa Temple, Haeinsa Temple, Jogyesa Temple, Beomeosa Temple, Bongeunsa Temple. These are just a few examples of the many beautiful temples that can be found throughout South Korea. Whether you are interested in history, religion, or just appreciate stunning architecture, a visit to one of South Korea's temples is sure to be a memorable experience.

3. Visit various theme parks in South Korea!

Theme Parks

South Korea has many popular theme parks that offer a wide range of attractions, from thrilling roller coasters to family-friendly rides and shows. Here are some of the most popular theme parks in South Korea: Lotte World, Everland, Seoul Land, Caribbean Bay, Daejeon O-World. These are just a few examples of the many theme parks that can be found throughout South Korea. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or just looking for a fun day out with family and friends, South Korea's theme parks offer something for everyone.
